## Review Instructions

**Dear Expert Reviewer,**

Thank you for participating in the validation of QuestionForge, an educational content quality assessment tool. Your expertise will help us ensure our automated scoring aligns with professional standards.

**Your Task:**
1. Review 30 Python programming questions (10 starter, 10 core, 10 stretch difficulty)
2. Rate each question on the criteria below using the provided rubrics
3. Provide overall quality score (1-5 scale)
4. Add comments for scores <4.0

**Time Required:** Approximately 2-3 hours

**Compensation:** $250-500 honorarium (based on your preference)
